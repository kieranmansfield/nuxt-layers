# Autumn Collection

A moody collection of photographs exploring the warmth and tranquility of the autumn season.

## About This Collection

Shot over several weeks across rural landscapes, this collection focuses on:

- **Color palettes** — Rich oranges, deep reds, and golden yellows
- **Atmosphere** — Fog, mist, and soft directional light
- **Texture** — Bark, stone, and fallen foliage
